# Financial Research

Install-ready OpenClaw agents for research-support workflows across public-equity idea intake, fundamental analysis, scenario design, market monitoring, event tracking, sector research, and research-operations coordination.

## Included role families
- `equity-analysis` — core single-name research workflow from intake through business quality, valuation, thesis maintenance, and risk review
- `screening-comparison` — idea discovery, peer comparison, factor context, variant framing, market maps, and relative-value review
- `market-monitoring` — macro, sector, policy, cross-asset, sentiment, and catalyst monitoring around covered names
- `event-earnings-monitoring` — preview, reaction, guidance-change, transcript, estimate-revision, and event-calendar coverage
- `portfolio-research-support` — exposure review, coverage coordination, watchlist curation, diligence tracking, and handoff editing for research teams
- `scenario-stress-analysis` — base/upside/downside scenarios, liquidity stress, sensitivity analysis, and catalyst-path pressure testing
- `sector-thematic-research` — sector structure, thematic trends, competitive landscapes, channel-check planning, KPI tracking, and supply-chain signal monitoring
- `research-ops-data-monitoring` — consensus tracking, alternative-data review, memo standardization, data quality, catalyst calendars, and evidence-gap management

## Boundary
These agents support research preparation and evidence organization. They do not replace licensed investment advice, fiduciary judgment, suitability determinations, or trade execution decisions.
