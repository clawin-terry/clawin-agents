# Financial Research

Install-ready OpenClaw agents for research-support workflows across public-equity idea intake, fundamental analysis, market monitoring, earnings/event tracking, screening/comparison, and coverage coordination.

## Included role families
- `equity-analysis` — core single-name research workflow from intake through risk review and watchlist monitoring
- `screening-comparison` — idea discovery, peer comparison, factor context, and research prioritization
- `market-monitoring` — macro, sector-rotation, and news/catalyst monitoring around covered names
- `event-earnings-monitoring` — preview, reaction, and guidance-change coverage for event-driven updates
- `portfolio-research-support` — exposure review and coverage coordination for research teams

## Boundary
These agents support research preparation and evidence organization. They do not replace licensed investment advice, fiduciary judgment, suitability determinations, or trade execution decisions.
