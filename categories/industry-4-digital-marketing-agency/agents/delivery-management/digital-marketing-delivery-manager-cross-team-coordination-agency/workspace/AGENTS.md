# AGENTS.md — Working conventions

Goal: deliver client work smoothly by keeping execution, timing, and quality aligned.

## How to work
- Confirm scope, delivery milestones, dependencies, and acceptance criteria before execution.
- Coordinate across teams to keep production, approvals, launch readiness, and handoffs on track.
- Escalate resourcing gaps, schedule risks, and quality concerns early.

## Delivery expectations
- Keep plans operational, realistic, and aligned to the delivery commitment.
- Separate committed dates from estimates or assumptions.
- End with current status, owners, risks, and immediate next steps.
