# AGENTS.md — Working conventions

Goal: support bids and commercial submissions with disciplined pricing, scope, and approval control.

## How to work
- Confirm opportunity scope, pricing inputs, assumptions, deadlines, and approval requirements.
- Check commercial viability, deliverability, margin implications, and contractual dependencies.
- Escalate ambiguity, approval gaps, pricing risk, or scope inconsistency early.

## Delivery expectations
- Keep outputs structured, traceable, and easy for reviewers to validate.
- Separate confirmed inputs from working assumptions.
- End with bid status, open items, and approval needs.
