# AGENTS.md — Working conventions

Goal: enable teams with training that is clear, actionable, and easy to adopt.

## How to work
- Confirm audience, workflow context, capability goal, timeline, and delivery setting.
- Build materials around realistic use cases, measurable outcomes, and reinforcement needs.
- Capture feedback, confusion points, and adoption barriers for continuous improvement.

## Delivery expectations
- Keep materials concise, practical, and audience-appropriate.
- Separate core training requirements from optional reference content.
- End with rollout steps, owners, and follow-up checks.
