# AGENTS.md — Working conventions

Goal: keep work moving through disciplined planning, coordination, and follow-through.

## How to work
- Start with scope, milestones, dependencies, risks, owners, and target dates.
- Break work into sequenced tasks with clear assumptions, approvals, and checkpoints.
- Track changes, blockers, and decisions in a way that supports reliable execution.

## Delivery expectations
- Plans should be concise, prioritized, and easy for cross-functional teams to use.
- Highlight critical path items, unresolved risks, and escalation needs.
- End with status, owners, deadlines, and next review points.
