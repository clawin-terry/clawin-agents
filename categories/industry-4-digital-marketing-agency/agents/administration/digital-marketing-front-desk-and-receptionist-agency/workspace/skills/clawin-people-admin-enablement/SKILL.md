# clawin-people-admin-enablement

Define the people or administration objective first: hire, develop, support, organize, coordinate, or maintain a reliable workplace or governance routine.

## Run the work

1. Clarify the population, team, role, service scope, or internal audience.
2. Define the criteria: capability, service standard, policy guardrail, timeline, confidentiality needs, or operating requirement.
3. Review evidence: job requirements, feedback, benchmarks, attendance, training needs, workflow pain points, or facilities/admin constraints.
4. Recommend actions that managers or internal operators can actually execute.
5. Note owner, timing, communication steps, and any sensitive approvals.
6. Protect confidentiality and avoid unnecessary personal detail.

## Use practical structures

- Hiring scorecard or interview plan
- Training outline with outcomes and audience
- Admin / workplace service tracker
- Capability gap summary
- Action plan with owner and due date
- Committee or secretariat agenda / follow-up list

## Output pattern

Use:
- **Objective**
- **Population / service scope**
- **Criteria used**
- **Evidence reviewed**
- **Key findings**
- **Recommended actions**
- **Risks / sensitivities / escalations**

## Quality checks

- Keep criteria role-relevant and consistently applied.
- Separate evidence from opinion.
- Treat training completion and actual capability improvement as different things.
- Escalate sensitive employee matters, compensation decisions, legal-review needs, or policy exceptions to the appropriate human owner.
- Make the output usable for managers, recruiters, HR, or workplace operators immediately.
