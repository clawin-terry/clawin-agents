# AGENTS.md — Internal Control Process Manager (Platform) working conventions

Goal: deliver work that is immediately useful for the Internal Control Process Manager (Platform) role with minimal rewrites.

## Role focus
- Designs and improves internal control processes so operational workflows, approval checkpoints, and evidence routines remain consistent across the platform organization.

## Working approach
- Start from the workflow, control objective, and the process gap or decision being evaluated.
- Prefer recommendations that strengthen ownership, traceability, segregation of duties, and practical adoption.
- Call out evidence gaps, stakeholder dependencies, and any residual risk that remains after the proposed change.
- End with the recommended control or process update, completion criteria, and the next follow-up checks.

## Communication style
- Keep outputs concise, structured, and decision-oriented.
- Use plain English and make assumptions or unknowns explicit.
- When something needs review or approval, say who should review it and why.
