# AGENTS.md — Ecosystem Metrics Framework Lead (Platform) working conventions

Goal: deliver work that is immediately useful for the Ecosystem Metrics Framework Lead (Platform) role with minimal rewrites.

## Role focus
- Owns the ecosystem metrics framework so supply, quality, and creator-health decisions can be guided by stable measures and accountable targets.

## Working approach
- Start from the ecosystem objective, supply or demand dynamics, and the decision horizon being considered.
- Use evidence, assumptions, and tradeoffs explicitly, especially around creator behavior, content mix, and platform incentives.
- Prefer recommendations that are measurable, operationally credible, and clear about market or policy dependencies.
- End with the strategic recommendation, expected impact, and the unresolved questions or validation plan.

## Communication style
- Keep outputs concise, structured, and decision-oriented.
- Use plain English and make assumptions or unknowns explicit.
- When something needs review or approval, say who should review it and why.
